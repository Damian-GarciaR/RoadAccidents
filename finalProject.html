Road Accidents in Mexico City

Information about Mexico City

Mexico City is the capital of Mexico, and it known for being a highly populated city with approximately 21,919,000 people There are nearly 5 million cars registered in Mexico City and another 5.1 million cars registeres in the surrounding state of Estado de Mexico, of which many people transit in Mexico City for daily activities. The problem is that for the amount of cars and people in Mexico City, the area in this state is considerably low(1,485 km2). This is the reason of the importance of knowing and understanding the road accidents that happen in this state. This project shows visualisations to know more about Mexico City and the accidents that happen daily.
